What is a System Action?

A "System Action" allows you to execute the following actions:

  • Log Out…
  • Switch to…
  • --------
  • Sleep
  • Reboot
  • Shutdown
  • --------
  • Empty Trash
  • --------
  • Mute
  • Volume Up
  • Volume Down
  • --------
  • Switch grayscale.
  • Invert Black & White.
  • --------
  • Launch Screen Saver (Mac OS X.4 and later)
